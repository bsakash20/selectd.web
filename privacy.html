Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Selectd ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information that we receive when you use our mobile application ("App").

2. Data Collection & Storage

Selectd is primarily a local-first application. We minimize data collection to the absolute essentials required for the App's functionality.

Locally Stored Data

The following data is stored exclusively on your device's internal storage (SQLite database):

  • Job application details (companies, roles, notes, status)
  • File names of uploaded resumes
  • Generated HTML export files
  • Reminder schedules

We do not have access to this data. It remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Analytics & Tracking

We may use limited analytics to improve our App's performance and understand usage patterns.

  • RevenueCat: We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions and in-app purchases. RevenueCat may collect an anonymous App User ID and purchase history to validate your subscription status.
  • Sentry: We use Sentry to collect anonymous crash reports and performance data. This helps us identify and fix bugs. Sentry may collect device information (model, OS version) and stack traces. No personal job data is sent to Sentry.

On iOS, we request your permission via App Tracking Transparency (ATT) before tracking your activity across other companies' apps and websites for advertising or data broker purposes. You can withdraw this consent at any time in your device settings.

3. Third-Party Services

We maintain a strict policy against sharing your data. However, the App relies on the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store & Google Play Store: Process all payments. We do not process or store your credit card information.
  • RevenueCat: User subscription management infrastructure.
  • Sentry: App monitoring and error tracking.

4. Data Deletion

You have full control over your data.

  • Delete All Data: You can permanently delete all locally stored data by navigating to Settings > Danger Zone > Delete All Data.
  • Uninstalling: Uninstalling the App will also remove all local data.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
